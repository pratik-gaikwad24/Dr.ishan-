About Dr. Ishan R. Shevate

Dr. Ishan R. Shevate is a highly experienced and fellowship-trained orthopedic surgeon specializing in shoulder and knee disorders, arthroscopic (keyhole) surgery, sports injuries, rotator cuff repair, ACL reconstruction, and joint preservation.
