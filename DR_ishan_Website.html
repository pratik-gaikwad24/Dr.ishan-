About Dr. Ishan Gajbe

Dr. Ishan Gajbe is a highly experienced and fellowship-trained orthopedic surgeon based in Baramati, Maharashtra, specializing in shoulder and knee disorders, arthroscopic (keyhole) surgery, sports injuries, rotator cuff repair, ACL reconstruction, and joint preservation.

With a patient-first approach, Dr. Gajbe is known for accurate diagnosis, advanced treatment, and compassionate care, helping patients in Baramati and surrounding areas return to active, pain-free lives.
